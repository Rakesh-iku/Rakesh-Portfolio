Minimal & Professional Background

This demo uses a fixed background so the page content scrolls normally. The animation is gentle and won't distract from text.

Scroll down to confirm the background stays behind content and scrolling works. If you prefer the particle background instead of the gradient, uncomment the canvas tag in the background wrapper and comment out the .soft-gradient div (instructions above).

To integrate this into your website: paste the .bg-wrap block at the top of your <body> and include the CSS. If you already have a global stylesheet, move the CSS there and keep only the markup in your HTML.
